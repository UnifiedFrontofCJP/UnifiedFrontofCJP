Our Mission

A Unified Front for Accountability

We are CJP, the new generation committed to bringing transparency and integrity back into public discourse. Our members unite to challenge misinformation and drive positive change for India.

  • Exposing false narratives with irrefutable data.
  • Conducting in-depth research on critical national issues.
  • Fostering a collaborative environment for members to act.
